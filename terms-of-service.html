2. How Vowed Works

Vowed lets you set personal goals, "stake" a virtual in-app currency ("coins") on those goals, and prove completion with a live photo taken through the app's camera. Other users ("jurors") review your photo and vote on whether it proves the goal was completed. If the majority of jurors approve, your staked coins are refunded to your balance. If your submission is rejected, the deadline passes without proof, or you fail to complete your goal, staked coins are not refunded.

Vowed also offers an AI Coach feature that provides suggestions and plans based on the messages you send it, and a Duel feature that lets you and another user each stake coins on a shared goal.
