Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Vowed ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: name, email address, and password (securely managed through our authentication provider, Supabase).
  • Goal data: the goals you create, including text descriptions, coin amounts staked, schedules, and completion status.
  • Photo proof: photos you take within the App to verify completion of your goals. These photos are stored securely and used only for verification purposes.
  • Usage data: basic app interaction data needed to operate core features (e.g. goal history, coin balance).
  • Device information: for the purpose of sending you local notifications and reminders you set up within the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To operate the core functionality of the App, including goal tracking, coin balance management, and proof verification.
  • To send reminder notifications you have scheduled.
  • To review and approve/reject photo proof submissions (manually, by our team) to ensure fairness in the coin refund process.
  • To respond to appeals submitted regarding rejected proof.

3. Photo Proof Storage

Photos you upload as proof of completing a goal are stored securely using our backend provider, Supabase Storage. These photos are used only to verify your goal completion and are not shared with third parties or used for any other purpose.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with the following service providers, solely to operate the App:

  • Supabase — for authentication, database, and file storage.
  • Expo — for push/local notification delivery infrastructure.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account and goal data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. To make such a request, contact us using the information below.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@vowedapp.com
